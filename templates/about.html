About OptiCrop

Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine

The Problem

Choosing the wrong crop for a plot of land leads to low yields, wasted water and fertilizer, and financial loss. Farmers must weigh soil nutrients, climate and seasonal conditions — a complex decision that OptiCrop makes simple and data-driven.

Our Approach

OptiCrop integrates key environmental factors — Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), temperature, humidity, pH and rainfall — and applies machine learning to recommend the most suitable crop for maximum yield and resource efficiency. The system is trained on a labelled agricultural dataset covering 22 crops and compares four algorithms, deploying the most accurate.

The Model

Four classifiers were trained and compared — K-Nearest Neighbors, Logistic Regression, Decision Tree and Random Forest — alongside a K-Means clustering analysis of soil patterns.

Impact

By matching crops to conditions, OptiCrop helps improve productivity and profitability, optimize the use of water and fertilizers, reduce crop-failure risk, and support sustainable, precision-farming practices — benefiting farmers, agribusiness, researchers and policymakers alike.
