## Problem-Solution Fit Canvas

### 1. Customer Segment(s)
Smallholder farmers; agricultural extension officers; new/hobby growers; agri-tech students.

### 2. Jobs to be Done / Problems
Decide the most suitable crop for a specific plot using its soil chemistry (N, P, K, pH) and
climate (temperature, humidity, rainfall) — quickly and confidently.

### 3. Triggers
Start of a sowing season; receiving a fresh soil-test report; a previous crop under-performing.

### 4. Emotions (before / after)
- **Before:** anxious, overwhelmed, uncertain.
- **After:** confident, reassured, empowered.

### 5. Available Solutions Today
Neighbour/word-of-mouth choices; costly/scarce expert consultation; generic web articles that
ignore the plot's actual readings.

### 6. Customer Constraints
Limited agronomy expertise; limited time and money; basic smartphone/browser only.

### 7. Behaviour
Copies what others plant; keeps but rarely acts on soil-test numbers; searches for generic advice.

### 8. Channels of Behaviour
Online search, community advice, agri-extension visits.

### 9. Problem Root Cause
There is no simple tool that maps measurable soil + climate inputs to a specific, trustworthy
crop recommendation with actionable guidance.

### 10. Your Solution
**OptiCrop** — a web app where a user enters 7 values and instantly receives the best-fit crop
(with confidence + top-3 alternatives) enriched with agronomic tips, backed by a Random Forest
model (99.55% accuracy), plus accounts and history.

## Fit Statement

> For **farmers and advisors** who need to **choose the right crop from soil and climate data**,
> OptiCrop provides an **instant, confidence-scored ML recommendation with practical guidance** —
> unlike generic advice or scarce expert consultation, it uses the plot's *own* measured values.
