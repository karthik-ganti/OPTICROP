  rehearsal were done collaboratively.
- **Team Lead coordination:** Vaddimani Nikitha Sai coordinated task allocation, merged
  contributions, and ensured the deployed build matched the documented behaviour.
- **End-to-end participation:** every member presents the part of the system they built, giving
  the audience an accurate view of individual contributions.
